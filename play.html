Side projects, experiments & things for fun.

I Believe in a Thing Called Love

As an animator, I collaborated with an illustrator to create this kinetic typography project in After Effects, inspired by "I Believe in a Thing Called Love" by The Darkness, blending animation and illustration to capture its bold and expressive themes.

UN/FOLD

A team-developed 3D adventure game built in Unity, featuring puzzles, platforming, and a fantasy world. As the game designer, I focused on the setting, level design, and mechanics, allowing players to shapeshift into humans, wolves, and seagulls to solve challenges creatively.

Market Street Booklet Kit

CCA team project for ULI San Francisco's Market Street Reimagined competition. Designed to revive foot traffic and support local businesses, the interactive booklet used maps, stamps, and coupons to guide people through Market Street landmarks. Exhibited at the Ferry Building (July 23 – Aug 8, 2025) and reviewed by a jury that included Jony Ive.
